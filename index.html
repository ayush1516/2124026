Dev Sanskriti Vishwavidyalaya

The university was established in the year 2002

The lush green campus is set in the Himalayan surroundings and is in the lap of nature on the bank of Ganga. The university has a well-equipped infrastructure in the form of an adequate number of lecture theatres with modern ICT facilities, laboratories, central and departmental libraries, auditoriums, sports and gym center, health center, amenities center, student and faculty cafeterias, fully wi-fi connectivity, adequate security arrangements, Yagya shala, herbal gardens, gaushala, multi-modal polyclinic, etc. It is a residential and non-affiliated university with co-education facilities having separate hostels for boys and girls along with a residential facility for all the faculty and supporting staff.
